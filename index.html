About Me

Hi, I'm Manish Dhumale, a 34-year-old MBA graduate from Mumbai. I own a startup called Pagal Awwrat, which makes quirky t-shirts for women and sells them online. I am very passionate about my work as it gives me a chance to be creative, and I love that. I enjoy people management and talking to customers, and brainstorming new ideas with my team for the business. I consider myself creative and witty at heart with a great sense of humor.

I enjoy gyming, cycling, swimming, trekking, and socializing with friends over coffee. I'm a foodie at times and humble with everyone. As an aspiring entrepreneur, I aim to brush up on my risk-taking skills to grow. I did theater before, so I like acting, singing, and learning to play the guitar. I call myself 'Aaramkhhor' because I can be lazy at times. I laugh a lot with my loved ones as they bring energy into my life. I tend to overthink and daydream, and I sometimes self-doubt, as every entrepreneur does.
